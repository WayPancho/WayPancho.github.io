泰勒展开的几种形式

数学书上最原始的泰勒展开形式应该是这样的

f(x0+Δx,y0+Δy)=f(x0,y0)+[∂f∂x(x0,y0)Δx+∂f∂y(x0,y0)Δy]+[∂2f∂x2(x0,y0)Δx2+2∂2f∂x∂y(x0,y0)ΔxΔy+∂2f∂y2(x0,y0)Δy2]+...f(x_{0} + \Delta x,y_{0} + \Delta y) \\ = f(x_{0},y_{0}) \\ + \left[ \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}(x_{0},y_{0})\Delta x + \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}(x_{0},y_{0})\Delta y\right] \\ + \left[ \frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial x^{2}}(x_{0},y_{0})\Delta x^{2} + 2\frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial x\partial y}(x_{0},y_{0})\Delta x \Delta y + \frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial y^{2}}(x_{0},y_{0})\Delta y^{2} \right]\\ +... f(x0​+Δx,y0​+Δy)=f(x0​,y0​)+[∂x∂f​(x0​,y0​)Δx+∂y∂f​(x0​,y0​)Δy]+[∂x2∂2f​(x0​,y0​)Δx2+2∂x∂y∂2f​(x0​,y0​)ΔxΔy+∂y2∂2f​(x0​,y0​)Δy2]+...

只有二阶混合偏导数存在且连续的函数，两个二阶混合偏导数才相等，这里默认了这一点。

第二项有时候会借助Hessian\mathrm{Hessian}Hessian矩阵

Hf=[∂2f∂x2∂2f∂y∂x∂2f∂x∂y∂2f∂y2]H_{f} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial x^{2}} & \frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial y\partial x} \\ \frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial x\partial y} & \frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial y^{2}} \end{bmatrix} Hf​=[∂x2∂2f​∂x∂y∂2f​​∂y∂x∂2f​∂y2∂2f​​]

然后用如下形式表达

[ΔxΔy]Hf[ΔxΔy]\begin{bmatrix} \Delta x & \Delta y \end{bmatrix} H_{f} \begin{bmatrix} \Delta x \\ \Delta y \end{bmatrix} [Δx​Δy​]Hf​[ΔxΔy​]

然而，这些形式都是展开后的繁琐形式，并不便于看出本质，因此想到引入右作用的算子概念，用微分算子

d=(Δx∂∂x+Δy∂∂y)d = \left( \Delta x \frac{\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac{\partial}{\partial y}\right) d=(Δx∂x∂​+Δy∂y∂​)

来描述微分操作，发现泰勒展开式可以写作

f(x0+Δx,y0+Δy)=f(x0,y0)+[Δx∂∂x(x0,y0)+Δy∂∂y(x0,y0)]f+[Δx2∂2∂x2(x0,y0)+2ΔxΔy∂2∂x∂y(x0,y0)+Δy2∂2∂y2(x0,y0)]f+...f(x_{0} + \Delta x,y_{0} + \Delta y) \\ = f(x_{0},y_{0}) \\ + \left[ \Delta x\frac{\partial }{\partial x}(x_{0},y_{0}) + \Delta y \frac{\partial }{\partial y}(x_{0},y_{0}) \right]f \\ + \left[ \Delta x^{2} \frac{\partial^{2} }{\partial x^{2}}(x_{0},y_{0}) + 2\Delta x \Delta y\frac{\partial^{2} }{\partial x\partial y}(x_{0},y_{0}) + \Delta y^{2} \frac{\partial^{2} }{\partial y^{2}}(x_{0},y_{0}) \right]f\\ +... f(x0​+Δx,y0​+Δy)=f(x0​,y0​)+[Δx∂x∂​(x0​,y0​)+Δy∂y∂​(x0​,y0​)]f+[Δx2∂x2∂2​(x0​,y0​)+2ΔxΔy∂x∂y∂2​(x0​,y0​)+Δy2∂y2∂2​(x0​,y0​)]f+...

由于二阶项形式上等于

[Δx2∂2∂x2(x0,y0)+2ΔxΔy∂2∂x∂y(x0,y0)+Δy2∂2∂y2(x0,y0)]f=[Δx∂∂x(x0,y0)+Δy∂∂y(x0,y0)]2f\left[ \Delta x^{2} \frac{\partial^{2} }{\partial x^{2}}(x_{0},y_{0}) + 2\Delta x \Delta y\frac{\partial^{2} }{\partial x\partial y}(x_{0},y_{0}) + \Delta y^{2} \frac{\partial^{2} }{\partial y^{2}}(x_{0},y_{0}) \right]f\\ =\left[ \Delta x\frac{\partial }{\partial x}(x_{0},y_{0}) + \Delta y \frac{\partial }{\partial y}(x_{0},y_{0}) \right]^{2} f [Δx2∂x2∂2​(x0​,y0​)+2ΔxΔy∂x∂y∂2​(x0​,y0​)+Δy2∂y2∂2​(x0​,y0​)]f=[Δx∂x∂​(x0​,y0​)+Δy∂y∂​(x0​,y0​)]2f

所以泰勒展开写作

f(x0+Δx,y0+Δy)=f(x0,y0)+[Δx∂∂x(x0,y0)+Δy∂∂y(x0,y0)]f+[Δx∂∂x(x0,y0)+Δy∂∂y(x0,y0)]2f+...f(x_{0} + \Delta x,y_{0} + \Delta y) \\ = f(x_{0},y_{0}) \\ + \left[ \Delta x\frac{\partial }{\partial x}(x_{0},y_{0}) + \Delta y \frac{\partial }{\partial y}(x_{0},y_{0}) \right]f \\ + \left[ \Delta x\frac{\partial }{\partial x}(x_{0},y_{0}) + \Delta y \frac{\partial }{\partial y}(x_{0},y_{0}) \right]^{2} f\\ +... f(x0​+Δx,y0​+Δy)=f(x0​,y0​)+[Δx∂x∂​(x0​,y0​)+Δy∂y∂​(x0​,y0​)]f+[Δx∂x∂​(x0​,y0​)+Δy∂y∂​(x0​,y0​)]2f+...

即

f(x0+Δx,y0+Δy)=f(x0,y0)+df+d2f+...f(x_{0} + \Delta x,y_{0} + \Delta y) = f(x_{0},y_{0}) +df + d^{2}f+... f(x0​+Δx,y0​+Δy)=f(x0​,y0​)+df+d2f+...

对比一元函数的泰勒展开

f(x+Δx)=f(x)+dfdxΔx+d2fdx2Δx2+...f(x+\Delta x) = f(x) + \frac{df}{dx} \Delta x + \frac{d^{2}f}{dx^{2}}\Delta x^{2} + ... f(x+Δx)=f(x)+dxdf​Δx+dx2d2f​Δx2+...

一方面，将其看作

f(x+Δx)=f(x)+(Δxddx)f+(Δx2d2dx2)f+...f(x+\Delta x) = f(x) + (\Delta x\frac{d}{dx})f + (\Delta x^{2}\frac{d^{2}}{dx^{2}})f + ... f(x+Δx)=f(x)+(Δxdxd​)f+(Δx2dx2d2​)f+...

与上面的多元展开形式

f(x0+Δx,y0+Δy)=f(x0,y0)+[Δx∂∂x(x0,y0)+Δy∂∂y(x0,y0)]f+[Δx∂∂x(x0,y0)+Δy∂∂y(x0,y0)]2f+...f(x_{0} + \Delta x,y_{0} + \Delta y) \\= f(x_{0},y_{0}) \\+\left[ \Delta x\frac{\partial }{\partial x}(x_{0},y_{0}) + \Delta y \frac{\partial }{\partial y}(x_{0},y_{0}) \right]f \\+ \left[ \Delta x\frac{\partial }{\partial x}(x_{0},y_{0}) + \Delta y \frac{\partial }{\partial y}(x_{0},y_{0}) \right]^{2} f\\+... f(x0​+Δx,y0​+Δy)=f(x0​,y0​)+[Δx∂x∂​(x0​,y0​)+Δy∂y∂​(x0​,y0​)]f+[Δx∂x∂​(x0​,y0​)+Δy∂y∂​(x0​,y0​)]2f+...

吻合；另一方面，如果认为泰勒展开是对于一个小量增量Δx\Delta xΔx展开，则Δx→dx\Delta x \to dxΔx→dx，假如直接从

f(x+Δx)=f(x)+dfdxΔx+d2fdx2Δx2+...f(x+\Delta x) = f(x) + \frac{df}{dx} \Delta x + \frac{d^{2}f}{dx^{2}}\Delta x^{2} + ... f(x+Δx)=f(x)+dxdf​Δx+dx2d2f​Δx2+...

中将Δx\Delta xΔx和dxdxdx“约掉”，一元函数泰勒展开形式就变为

f(x+Δx)=f(x)+df+d2f+...f(x+\Delta x) = f(x) + df + d^{2}f + ... f(x+Δx)=f(x)+df+d2f+...

这与上面的

f(x0+Δx,y0+Δy)=f(x0,y0)+df+d2f+...f(x_{0} + \Delta x,y_{0} + \Delta y) = f(x_{0},y_{0}) +df + d^{2}f+... f(x0​+Δx,y0​+Δy)=f(x0​,y0​)+df+d2f+...

也是吻合的。于是，可以看出微分算子

d=(Δx∂∂x+Δy∂∂y)d = \left( \Delta x \frac{\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac{\partial}{\partial y}\right) d=(Δx∂x∂​+Δy∂y∂​)

恰当的描述了多元函数的微分，形式

f(x0+Δx,y0+Δy)=f(x0,y0)+df+d2f+...f(x_{0} + \Delta x,y_{0} + \Delta y) = f(x_{0},y_{0}) +df + d^{2}f+... f(x0​+Δx,y0​+Δy)=f(x0​,y0​)+df+d2f+...

已经相当好理解。

然而，在物理中更多使用的是矢量形式，借助nabla\mathrm{nabla}nabla算子（求导算符）

∇=∂∂xx^+∂∂yy^\nabla = \frac{\partial }{\partial x} \hat{x} + \frac{\partial }{\partial y} \hat{y} ∇=∂x∂​x^+∂y∂​y^​

将自变量看作矢量x=(x1,x2)x = (x_{1},x_{2})x=(x1​,x2​)，将x0=(x10,x20)x^{0} = (x^{0}_{1},x^{0}_{2})x0=(x10​,x20​)当作展开处的点，将Δx=(Δx1,Δx2)\Delta x = (\Delta x_{1},\Delta x_{2})Δx=(Δx1​,Δx2​)看作增量，发现微分算符

d=(Δx∂∂x+Δy∂∂y)=(Δx1,Δx2)⋅(∂∂x,∂∂y)=Δx⋅∇d = \left( \Delta x \frac{\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac{\partial}{\partial y}\right) \\ = (\Delta x_{1},\Delta x_{2}) \cdot (\frac{\partial}{\partial x},\frac{\partial}{\partial y})\\ = \Delta x \cdot \nabla d=(Δx∂x∂​+Δy∂y∂​)=(Δx1​,Δx2​)⋅(∂x∂​,∂y∂​)=Δx⋅∇

于是将

f(x0+Δx,y0+Δy)=f(x0,y0)+df+d2f+...f(x_{0} + \Delta x,y_{0} + \Delta y) = f(x_{0},y_{0}) +df + d^{2}f+... f(x0​+Δx,y0​+Δy)=f(x0​,y0​)+df+d2f+...

改写为

f(x0+Δx)=f(x0)+(Δx⋅∇)f+(Δx⋅∇)2f+...f(x^{0} + \Delta x) = f(x^{0}) +(\Delta x \cdot \nabla)f + (\Delta x \cdot \nabla)^{2}f+... f(x0+Δx)=f(x0)+(Δx⋅∇)f+(Δx⋅∇)2f+...

成为一个新形式的表达式。
